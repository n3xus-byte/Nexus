FAQ

Common questions.

Where do I get the loadstring? ▾
The loadstring is available in the #scripts channel inside our Discord server. Join the server first, then copy it from there.
How do I get a key? ▾
You can get a key via LootLabs using the button above, or by asking in the #get-key channel in our Discord. Both options are completely free.
How long does a key last? ▾
Keys are valid for 24 hours from the moment they are generated. After that, simply get a new one — it takes under 2 minutes.
Is LootLabs safe? ▾
Yes. LootLabs is a well-known and trusted key system used by many Roblox script communities. It never asks for passwords or personal information.
My key is not working — what do I do? ▾
Make sure you copied the full key with no extra spaces. If it still does not work, open a ticket in our Discord and a staff member will sort it out for you.
