Get Your Key

Follow these steps to unlock access to Nexus scripts.

Access to Nexus scripts is free — you just need to grab a key from LootLabs. It takes about 2 minutes and helps support the community.

01

Click the button below

This takes you to our LootLabs key page. LootLabs is a safe, trusted key system used by many script communities.

02

Complete the checkpoints

LootLabs will show you a few short steps (usually just visiting a link or watching an ad for a few seconds). Complete all of them.

03

Copy your key

After completing the checkpoints, your unique key will be revealed. Copy it — it looks something like NEXUS-XXXX-XXXX-XXXX.

04

Enter it in Discord

Join our Discord server and use the /key command in the #key-verify channel. Paste your key and you'll get instant access to the script loader.

FAQ

Common questions.

How long does a key last? ▾
Keys are valid for 24 hours. You'll need to get a new one each day — this helps us keep things running smoothly.
Is LootLabs safe? ▾
Yes. LootLabs is a well-known and trusted key system used by many Roblox script communities. It never asks for personal information.
My key isn't working — what do I do? ▾
Make sure you copied the full key without extra spaces. If it still doesn't work, open a ticket in our Discord and a staff member will help you.
Do I need a key every day? ▾
Yes, keys expire daily. The LootLabs process takes under 2 minutes so it shouldn't be a big inconvenience.
