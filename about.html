About Nexus

Who we are and what drives us.

Our Story

Built by players, for players.

Nexus started as a small group of Roblox enthusiasts who were tired of bloated, buggy scripts that broke every update. We decided to build something better — clean, unified Lua scripts focused on FPS games.

Today we're a growing community of scripters, players, and testers who collaborate daily to keep the codebase sharp and game-compatible.

🛡️

Our Mission

Provide the cleanest, most reliable Lua scripts for Roblox FPS games — free to access with a key, maintained actively by the community.

01

Clean Code

We obsess over readable, optimized Lua. No obfuscated bloat — just code that works.

02

Community First

Everything we build is for the community. Feedback shapes every update we ship.

03

Always Updated

When games patch, we patch. Our scripts stay compatible across game updates.

04

FPS Focused

We specialize in shooting games — ESP, HBE and Aimlock tuned for FPS mechanics.
