Features

Everything in one place.

👁

ESP — Enemy Highlight

Renders enemy information through walls and obstacles. Fully customizable colors, box style, and visibility options.

  • ✦ Bounding boxes
  • ✦ Health bars
  • ✦ Name tags
  • ✦ Distance indicator
  • ✦ Skeleton ESP (select games)
🎯

Aimlock — Precision Targeting

Smooth, configurable aimlock that locks onto the nearest target in your FOV. Designed to feel natural.

  • ✦ FOV circle indicator
  • ✦ Adjustable smoothness
  • ✦ Head / body target options
  • ✦ Keybind toggle
  • ✦ Team check
💥

HBE — Hitbox Expander

Expands enemy hitboxes to increase your effective hit rate. Works silently alongside other features.

  • ✦ Custom hitbox size multiplier
  • ✦ Compatible with Aimlock
  • ✦ Toggle on/off instantly
